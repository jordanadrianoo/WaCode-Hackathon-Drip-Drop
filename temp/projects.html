Irrigation Project

This simple project can help you conserve water by automating the task of watering your lawn. This system will water your lawn at 5 am, minimizing evaporation. It also detects for humidity, ensuring that the lawn is only watered if it is dry. This automates the process of watering your lawn, giving you a simple, hands-on way to make a difference! Combine this irrigation system with native plants from your area and drip irrigation hoses for a sustainable yard that minimizes your water consumption!
